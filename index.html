Summary of My Findings

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


For creativity and physiological change, I practiced in singing for 15 mins everyday. The choice of song is based on my mood at that day. Throughout the 10-day practice, I found it is not easy to do the same thing almost everyday since I got very busy for school, but sometimes, doing the thing that I really enjoy, is a good way to relax and a good way to make my happy.
